My profession

I am a professional Occupational Therapist, an OT's primary goal is to promote the independence of clients by assisting them in performing ADL's.

We also help in rehabilitating patients in using assistive devices so they regain their independence quickly.

In addition to these we teach about Ergonomics and posture. OT's work in hospitals, clinics, and workplaces.
